Computer Science & Mathematics

I am a senior at the University of Michigan double majoring in Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Physics with a minor in Computer Science. I am fascinated by the beautiful mathematics behind AI&ML systems. In particular, I am interested in understanding how simple rules can give rise to complex behaviors - whether that's in physics, mathematical systems, or modern AI. I think this perspective is especially relevent to the question of how we can ensure AI systems remain reliable and beneficial as they become more powerful, which I believe is an extremely important question. To this end, I am applying to Computer Science masters programs to transition to research in AI Safety.

Currently working as a Data Science Fellow at the UM Center for Academic Innovation
