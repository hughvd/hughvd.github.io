Hugh Flournoy Van Deventer V

I am a senior at the University of Michigan double majoring in Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Physics with a minor in Computer Science. I am fascinated by the beautiful mathematics behind AI&ML systems. In particular, I am interested in understanding how simple rules can give rise to complex behaviors - whether that's in physics, mathematical systems, or modern AI. I think this perspective is especially relevent to the question of how we can ensure AI systems remain reliable and beneficial as they become more powerful, which I believe is an extremely important question.

From Interests to Insights: An LLM Approach to Course Recommendations Using Natural Language Queries
Andrew Lee, Hugh Van Deventer, Christopher Brooks
Under Review at LAK, 2025
We propose a novel methodology for course recommendations that leverages large language models to bridge the gap between students' natural language expressions of interests and formal course descriptions. Through extensive evaluation, we demonstrate that our approach not only provides more relevant recommendations but also offers interpretable explanations for its suggestions.
